Comfort-first design notes

Balanced typography, reduced-motion awareness, better spacing, higher legibility, and normalized simulation timing keep the experience calm across laptops, desktops, phones, and high-refresh-rate displays.

Tip: the bird is forgiving. Tap lightly, hold Brake on a phone (or Shift on a keyboard) to soften descents, and play for flow.
